eCycle Mobile App

UX/UI Case Study

eCycle is a mobile app that helps user to rent electrical bikes conveniently with ease. It provides tutorials on how to use the app and the docking stations, trac laws, safety rules and choosing the length of time for renting step by step. It also displays the e-bike stations based on user’s current location on the map. Users can also view how many e-bikes and docks are available through this app. Renting through the app is faster than the kiosk because the user can save their payment info and pay through the app in advance.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Users often walk to the e-bike rent station and find out that there is no available e-bikes to rent. So, every time it happens, a user needs to go to another nearby station with no assurance. They are not sure about the exact locations of the stations especially if they are not local. They end up searching for the location through Google map or e-bike’s ocial website. Also, they want to check the number of available bikes and docks without actually walking there. They also feel that the process of renting an e-bike from a kiosk was complicated and time consuming. Sometimes there are even long lines at the bike kiosk. It would be helpful if there was a simpler way to rent a bike in advance.

SOLUTION

The app can guide the user on how to rent e-bikes with an intuitive on-boarding process. Once the user is familiar in the process of renting a bike, the app can provide useful information such as location of the rental stations and the availability of the bikes. It will use the user’s current location to display the closest station or they can search an area for the stations. It also simplifies the process of users renting an e-bike. Rather than going to a kiosk every time and entering your information, credit card and duration, you can save time by using the app that saves the user’s profile. When you rent a bike with the app, you will receive a passcode to unlock the bike which can be used within an hour. Renting a bicycle becomes easy to do at home or on the way ahead of time with a mobile app.
